Who I Am

About Me

I'm a Computer Science & Engineering student at Daffodil International University (2025–2028), with a deep passion for Artificial Intelligence — particularly Large Language Models (LLMs).

My journey started with Python and data analysis, quickly evolving into local LLM deployment using Ollama, studying transformer architectures, and experimenting with model fine-tuning. I'm an active researcher in two university clubs, building the theoretical and practical foundations for my long-term ambition: building my own LLM from scratch before graduation.

I believe in learning by doing — my projects are proof of that philosophy, bridging theory with real-world implementation.
